Use case: Development Overseas

Your project has just started a new development cycle. The Cutomer Account GUI is to be changed, the relevant database tables are about to get new columns. Highly sensitive data, no developer should see your live data. As the spec is ready, the work is ready to be outsourced. What data do you provide to the developers? Live data with non disclosure agreements? Compliance will not agree. Create mock data of 200 cutomers?

Your live database has 12 million customers, the GUI will not be tested properly.

Enter Anonimecs. Take your production data, anomimise the relevant tables, and your developent team is ready to go on the anonimised database. Customers now have false addesses, last names are rehuffled, account balances altered.

Use case: User Acceptance Test

The new verion of your bank product is ready. Your testers are hot to check all the bells and whistles are in place. As they sit in a low cost location, and your local bank regulator forbids shipping data oversea, your default option is to move your tester in your legal jurisdiction, and live with the costs.

Enter Anonimecs. Create a schedule to copy your daily production data, anonimise the content. Mr Black now becomes Ms White, the account balances may be kept, the bottom line sums are still fine. And the testers need not to pack winter jackets.

Use case: External data analysis

You have agreed to take that external salary survey, that will provide you with aggregated data. And now you need to provide your sensitive data to an external database. Your data security official is giving you hard time, and he has a good reason for it.

Enter Anonimecs. Take your salary sheet, reshuffle the names, encode the identification, and the data are good to leave the house.
